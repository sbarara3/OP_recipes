Pizza:


Description:

Here's a quick and easy pizza recipe that will have have all your friends swooning over your cooking talents and rushing for the DNA test to confirm your italian heritage. Fun fact: If you eat a pizza whole, it only counts as one slice!


Cooking Time: 30 mins

Feeds: 2-3 people


What you need:

  • 1 cup Flour
  • 2 tbsp yeast
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 2-3 tomatoes
  • 5 lbs shredded cheese
  • 5-6 basil leaves

Steps:

  1. Combine sugar and yeast in about 200 ml of water and let sit for 15 mins
  2. Pour yeast water in flour and mix till a sticky dough forms
  3. Knead dough until firm and springy to the touch
  4. Channel all your skills into spinning the dough out into a proper base
  5. Put 2-3 literal whole tomatoes on the base
  6. Dump 5 pounds of cheese on that bad boy
  7. Bake at approximately 6 million kelvin for 0.0000015 seconds (much more efficient)
  8. Enjoy warm
